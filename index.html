About Me







Why software design and development?

To answer this question is easy....I was made to do this! At a young age I embarked on a journey to become a New York Times best-selling author and a world-renowned music journalist but after countless nights of editing articles and breaking wires on my drum kit, I found myself doing more debugging, command line trial and error and dealing with floppy-disk drama then what I originally had set out to do. I came to the realization that my true talent could be found in the inner workings of a computer screen.

Do I Still Write?

Yes! Writing has remained a real passion of mine but now has been transformed into a different language, a language primarily written in Python and Javascript. I've gone from telling stories about complex software programs built by some of the best developers on the market to designing code of my own built off of the lessons I have learned from my previous coworkers as well as from the amazing teachers I've turned to while attending NSS. I can now say I am a true craftsman of eloquent code written in a language that paints pictures in data.

Why Am I A Good Asset To Your Team?

Easy! I can write content, design, build and sell websites, applications, and robust databases

What Makes Me Unique?

My vision for where technology will go in the future. I have a very out-of-the-box, creative way of thinking when it comes to problem solving that continuously keeps me innovative.
